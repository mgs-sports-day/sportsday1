About

This website was developed to allow spectators of the Manchester Grammar School's annual Senior Sports Day to view results, events, and competitors in these events. It is a collaborative effort between the Computer Science and PE departments of the school, the staff responsible in each department being Mr D Millington and Mr T Grainger respectively. It is hoped that students, parents and staff will find this website beneficial. It works by fetching score and competitor data from a Google Drive spreadsheet, and displaying it in an easy-to-use and clear fashion.

How it works

This website is powered by AngularJS and Google Sheets. We query a Google Sheet with lots of live data in it every time a page is loaded, using an XMLHttpRequest();, using the Google Sheets API v4. All calculation of scores and results occurs in Google Sheets: not on the website. (For the curious, the full, public Google Sheet is avaliable here: FIX ME.) We then use various algorithms to compile the data into neatly laid-out tables, cross-referencing each other for easy navigation. The website is styled by Bootstrap. It's made to be as compact as possible so that it loads quickly on phones and doesn't use up 'too much data'. It's entirely responsive, and was tested mobile-first.

Cookies

This site (excluding Google Analytics, see below) stores 2 cookies on your device. Cookies are small text files placed on your device - only you can view them. The cookies this site uses are:

  • stopSurveyAlert: Used to determine if we should show you a message asking you to fill out a user experience survey. When you first visit the site, this cookie is set to false. If, in the "How are you finding the site?" dialog (which appears after you have visited 10 pages), you select either option, this cookie is set to true, so we know not to ask you about the survey again. This cookie is stored on your device for 6 hours after the time it was set.
  • cookieconsent_status: Used to determine if you have agreed to the cookie statment, so we do not show it to you again. This cookie is set to dismiss when you click "Got it!" on the cookie banner. This cookie is stored on your device for 24 hours after the time it was set.

Privacy

This site uses the service Google Analytics to collect information about those who visit it. Google Analytics works by placing 'cookies' (small text files) on your device to identify you as a user to them. Your data is stored by Google's servers for 26 months from your most recent visit to the site. By using this site, it is assumed that you agree to this. We advise that all users read the Google Analytics Privacy and Cookie policy here: http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/. If you do not want this information to be collected by us, please install the official Google Analytics opt-out extension here: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
