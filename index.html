MGS Sports Day Website: Home

Welcome to the MGS Senior Sports Day Website for 2018. On this site, you can view results in real time, view events in real time, and find out when it's time for you to compete.

It is intended for use only on Sports Day, and will be deactivated before and afterwards. This year, MGS Senior Sports Day will fall on 4th July 2018. You can view the current activation status of parts of the site below.

This site is developed internally by The Manchester Grammar School, and is a joint venture between the Computer Science and PE departments. Please read the About page for further information.
